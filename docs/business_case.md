# Business Case

## Problem Statement

Retail leadership needs a clear view of revenue growth, customer retention, operational quality, and margin pressure. Raw transactional data alone is not decision-ready; it must be transformed into reliable metrics and executive-friendly reporting.

## Objective

Build a production-quality analytics project that simulates the work of a business analyst at a large e-commerce company.

## Why This Matters

- E-commerce businesses operate on thin margins and high fulfillment complexity.
- Leadership needs timely visibility into revenue, delivery performance, seller quality, and customer behavior.
- Poorly managed retention or logistics quickly erodes growth quality.

## What RetailIQ Delivers

- Clean and reusable data pipeline
- Business-ready feature tables
- KPI engine for management reporting
- SQL analytics layer for repeatable reporting
- Executive dashboard blueprint for leadership consumption
- Senior management insights memo

## Strategic Value

RetailIQ helps leadership answer four core questions:

1. Is revenue growing?
2. Is growth profitable and operationally healthy?
3. Which customers, sellers, categories, and regions matter most?
4. What actions will improve performance next?

## Expected Outcomes

- Better growth visibility
- Improved retention strategy
- Stronger fulfillment governance
- More disciplined category and seller management
- Executive reporting that is actionable, not descriptive
